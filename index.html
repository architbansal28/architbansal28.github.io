Hello! I'm Archit Bansal. Welcome to my webpage.






Hi! I'm Archit, a first year undergraduate student pursuing my degree in Textile Technology from Indian Institute of Technology Delhi, India.

I have worked as the Chief Executive Officer and Managing Director of Think Green To Clean, a non-profit organisation working as a think tank for a cleaner, greener and better environment. I have also been the brand ambassador of TERI - The Energy and Resources Institute.

I am little introvert but I believe that introverts make good leaders. I am passionate about learning new skills and excel in problem solving and analytical skills. I have a keen interest in coding and development too.

I also enjoy art and craft, photography, music and sports. I was a part of the school music band. I am a regular tennis player and the Vice-Captain of Karakoram House Lawn Tennis team, IIT Delhi.

I am born and brought up in Chandigarh, India. I completed my schooling from St. Kabir Public School, Chandigarh. I was the House Captain in school in Class 10 and House Vice-Captain in Class 9, during which I got to learn a lot of team building, management and leadership skills.
